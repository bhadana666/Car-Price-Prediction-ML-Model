# Car-Price-Prediction-ML-Model
A Chinese automobile company wants to enter the American market by setting up a manufacturing unit and producing cars locally. To understand the factors affecting car pricing in the American market, they have contracted an automobile consulting company. The consulting company will analyze market trends, production costs, pricing strategies of competitors, brand image, and reputation to provide insights into the American market. By understanding these factors, the Chinese automobile company can develop an effective pricing strategy and establish itself as a strong competitor in the market.
